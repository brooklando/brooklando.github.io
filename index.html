I’m a Sophomore at Michigan State University studying Information Science, projected to graduate in December 2024. I currently have an Associates of General Studies, received from Macomb Community College under an academic concentration towards Psychology.

Why UX?

Growing up, I always knew that I wanted a career that would allow me to help others. When I began working towards my Associates, I came to realize that I didn’t have any idea how I wanted to help people. Due to my fondness for computers, I initially concentrated my studies towards Computer Science, but came to realize it’s a field that doesn’t allow creativity to flourish in the way that I'd hope. After lots of exploration, I found the industry a UX, and it's made its way as a significant part of my life and the drive towards my future.
